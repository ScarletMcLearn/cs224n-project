Re-enter Prince John of the Lord Chief-Justice; Officers with them Lord Chief-Justice Go, carry Sir John Falstaff to the Fleet: Take all his company along with him. Give Master Bardolph some wine, Davy. Second Drawer Sirrah, here will be the prince and Master Poins anon; and they will put on two of our jerkins and aprons; and Sir John must not know of it: Bardolph hath brought word. The Duke of Lancaster and Westmoreland; Against the Welsh, himself and Harry Monmouth: But who is substituted 'gainst the French, have no certain notice. Fear no colours: go with me to dinner: come, Lieutenant Pistol; come, Bardolph: shall be sent for soon at night.
