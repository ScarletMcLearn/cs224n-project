Enter and Clown Nay, prithee, put on this gown and this beard; make him believe thou art Sir Topas the curate: do it quickly; I'll call Sir Toby the whilst. In hope it shall not, Most freely confess, myself and Toby Set this device against Malvolio here, Upon some stubborn and uncourteous parts We had conceived against him: Maria writ The letter at Sir Toby's great importance; In recompense whereof he hath married her. Alas, Malvolio, this is not my writing, Though, confess, much like the character But out of question 'tis Maria's hand. Clown He is but mad yet, madonna; and the fool shall look to the madman.
