Re-enter My liege, the Duke of Buckingham is taken; That is the best news: that the Earl of Richmond Is with a mighty power landed at Milford, Is colder tidings, yet they must be told. Buckingham, now do play the touch, To try if thou be current gold indeed Young Edward lives: think now what would say. Enter and Sir Christopher, tell Richmond this from me: That in the sty of this most bloody boar My son George Stanley is frank'd up in hold: If revolt, off goes young George's head; The fear of that withholds my present aid.
