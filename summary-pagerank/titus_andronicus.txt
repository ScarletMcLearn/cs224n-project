Rome. What shall we do? and thou hast kill'd him. who comes here? Shall speak for thee? come, let me see it. How now! Exeunt Rome. But who comes with our brother Marcus here? Well hast thou lesson'd us; this shall we do. Lavinia, come. She is deliver'd, lords; she is deliver'd. Ay, that's my boy! What wouldst thou have us do, Andronicus? wouldst thou speak with us? Why, didst thou not come from heaven? Good Lord, how like the empress' sons they are! Come, Marcus, let us go. for thou hast stay'd us here too long. My hand shall go.
