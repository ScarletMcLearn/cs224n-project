Exeunt Antipholus of Syracuse and Dromio of Syracuse to the Priory Enter the Lady Abbess, Be quiet, people. Exeunt Antipholus of Syracuse and Antipholus of Ephesus There is a fat friend at your master's house, That kitchen'd me for you to-day at dinner: She now shall be my sister, not my wife.
