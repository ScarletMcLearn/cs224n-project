'Give my roan horse a drench,' says he; and answers 'Some fourteen,' an hour after; 'a trifle, a trifle.' prithee, call in Falstaff: I'll play Percy, and that damned brawn shall play Dame Mortimer his wife. To-morrow, good Sir Michael, is a day Wherein the fortune of ten thousand men Must bide the touch; for, sir, at Shrewsbury, As am truly given to understand, The king with mighty and quick-raised power Meets with Lord Harry: and, fear, Sir Michael, What with the sickness of Northumberland, Whose power was in the first proportion, And what with Owen Glendower's absence thence, Who with them was a rated sinew too And comes not in, o'er-ruled by prophecies, fear the power of Percy is too weak To wage an instant trial with the king.
